Visitor Pattern Implementation

In this case make sure to copy all the scripts across and modify them to your example.

With the use of abstract classes you could do some interesting things.

You can pass the same visitor to visit different classes in different ways.
-> Snow could do more damage to frost people.
-> Blinding grenades could disable UI for Human Players, and change ai direction states in NPCs.

Good for one time effects/modifications
